Linear Gradients

background-image: linear-gradient(direction, color-stop1, color-stop2, ...);

-Top to Bottom {linear-gradient(red, yellow)}

-to right

-to left

-to bottom left/to left bottom

-(angle): 0deg-bottom, 90deg-left, 180deg-top {linear-gradient(0deg, red, yellow)}

-Transparency (mo dan): gia tri cuoi tu 0->1 {linear-gradient(to right, rgba(255,0,0,0), rgba(255,0,0,1))}

-repeating-linear-gradient(red 25%, yellow 50%)

Radial Gradients

background-image: radial-gradient(shape size at position, start-color, ..., last-color);

-radial-gradient(red 5%, yellow 15%, green 60%);

- hinh tron; circle

-repeating-radial-gradient(red 5%, yellow 15%, green 60%)
